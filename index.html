Characters

Main & supporting characters

MOODi

The Mad Scientist

His talent is wanted from all planets throughout the galaxy. Ever since he was a toddler, he continued to create groundbreaking inventions to one day turn the scientific knowledge of the entire universe into data.

HEATHER

The First Princess

Heather is the first heir to Planet Muddy. She is smart and wise, she is the eldest daughter trusted by the king and queen. When feeling down, she seeks Moodi for comfort.

M.E.R

Moodi's robot pet

MER is Moody's first robot pet. It is equipped with artificial intelligence and able to machine learn to self-develop. In times of dangerous situations, its top-tier capacity demonstrates itself to help out Moodi and his friends.
